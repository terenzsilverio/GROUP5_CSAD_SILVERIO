The Silent Patient

Alex Michaelides

“...we often mistake love for fireworks - for drama and dysfunction. But real love is very quiet, very still. It's boring, if seen from the perspective of high drama. Love is deep and calm - and constant.”

A gripping psychological thriller that explores the complex mind of a woman named Alicia Berenson. Alicia, a renowned painter, is living a seemingly perfect life with her devoted husband, Gabriel. However, one fateful evening, Alicia shoots Gabriel in the face multiple times and then stops speaking altogether. She becomes a patient at the Grove, a secure psychiatric facility, where she is known as "the silent patient."
