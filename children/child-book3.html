Where the Wild Things Are (1963) by Maurice Sendak is a children's picture book that follows Max, a young boy dressed in a wolf costume, as he conjures up a wild adventure in his bedroom. At the beginning of the story, Max is in his wolf costume hammering knotted clothes to the wall and chasing his dog with a fork.
